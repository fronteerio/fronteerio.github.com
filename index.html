Crafting awesome experiences

Fronteer is a blend of product designers, software engineers and entrepeneurs. We conceptualize, design, build and provide useful and uplifting educational technology products and services. We also provide consulting services to primarily higher education institutions but have experience in all sectors.

Fronteer's foundations consist of a talented and renowed team of experts that combine high quality user-centred design with modern, multi-tenant and highly scalable architectures.

We're very proud of the products we've made too.

Ally

Ally is a revolutionary course content accessibility service that integrates closely with the Learning Management System. Driven by machine learning, it brings immediate benefits to students and instructors and helps institutions reach their accessibility goals.

ally.ac

Cumulus LRS

Cumulus LRS is a student-centred, hyper-efficient, multi-tenant and almost infinitely scalable Amazon Web Services-based Learning Record Store. Compatible with both the xAPI and IMS Caliper specifications, it provides flexible data access and delivers a range of unique student privacy features.

Embdr

Embdr is a powerful and easy-to-integrate REST-API-as-a-Service that can enhance your app with thumbnailing, high fidelity in-browser previews, metadata extraction and extraordinary content analytics for almost any type of content.

embdr.io

Open Academic Environment

The Apereo Open Academic Environment is an award-winning, multi-tenant and highly scalable platform for academic collaboration and networking. Recognized for its outstanding User Experience, it is in use at 100s of institutions around the world.

oaeproject.org

Timetable

Our multi-tenant Timetable system used by the University of Cambridge with a fast and efficient administor experience and renowed as a student favorite, allowing them to easily assemble their personal timetable.

timetable.cam.ac.uk

Collabosphere

Remixed content meets learning analytics meets gamification in Collabosphere, a suite of LTI tools designed to increase student engagement and offer new pedagogical opportunities, including an Asset Library, collaborative whiteboards and a student engagement leaderboard.
